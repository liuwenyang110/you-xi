1. Open `frontend-uniapp/` in HBuilderX or uni-app CLI.
2. Confirm `api/request.js` points to `http://localhost:8080/api/v1`.
3. Start with `/pages/common/login`.
4. Demo farmer flow:
   - login
   - create demand
   - matching
   - contact confirm
   - order detail
5. Demo owner flow:
   - login
   - owner home
   - dispatch detail
   - accept or reject
