- Database: `nongzhushou`

## One Full Demo Path

1. Open the login page.
2. Click `Test Farmer Login`.
3. On `Farmer Home`, click `Create Demand`.
4. Keep the default fields and click `Submit`.
5. The page jumps to `Matching`, and a new demand is created.
6. Open `Test Owner Login`.
7. On `Owner Home`, open the pending dispatch detail for attempt `1`.
8. Call or trigger `accept` for the dispatch.
9. The dispatch moves to `WAIT_FARMER_CONFIRM`, and a contact session is created.
10. Return to farmer side and open `Contact Confirm`.
11. Click `Confirm Contact`.
12. The page jumps to `Order Detail`, and order `1` is created.
